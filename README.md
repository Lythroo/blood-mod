**Simple Blood** adds realistic blood particle effects to Minecraft. When entities take damage, they bleed proportionally to the damage dealt - weak hits produce small, brief splashes while heavy hits create dramatic blood bursts. Entities also drip blood when at low health, and larger death bursts occur when they're killed.

---

Fully configurable through [Mod Menu](https://modrinth.com/mod/modmenu)
